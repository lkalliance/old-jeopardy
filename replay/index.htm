Welcome to the Ringnut Rerun, and opportunity for anyone who missed the original webcast of Ringnut Jeopardy to catch it as it unfolded, or if you've already seen it, to experience the showdown between CTS-1, Dirtrum and Lord Natrone once again!

We have two versions of the Ringnut Rerun for you: manual-advance and auto-advance. Choose manual advance if you'd like to control the pace of the game yourself. Choose auto-advance if you'd just like to kick back and let your web browser handle everything for you. Auto-advance features a pause button as well, and if you're so inclined, you can switch back and forth between modes at your leisure.
