CTS-1
What could be said about CTS-1 that hasn't already been said, mostly by frustrated trolls on the message boards? Recently named the winner of the Sayulita Annual Wet T-Shirt Championship, he's made a reputation on the boards for his insightful comments into the Elder Days and is thus considered one of the top experts around on The Silmarillion. CTS made national headlines in 2003 by becoming the first man to ever summit Mt. Everest with one eye closed.

LORD NATRONE
One would think that giving up his cushy scientific research position for the rough-and-tumble world of bartending would tell you all you need to know about Lord Natrone. But that would be leaving out his meticulous eye for detail in all things Tolkien and his California state record for water pistol skeet shooting (sub-quart class). Lord Natrone's Tolkien knowledge is limited only by the amount of material available. His knowledge of life, however, is boundless.

DIRTRUM
Asked by Larry King in a recent interview to describe King Dirtrum I in two words or less, Henry Kissinger responded: "bran muffin." I can think of no more fitting description. Recently named the runner-up in the Sayulita Annual Wet T-Shirt Championship, Dirtrum's grasp of Tolkien's works is surpassed only by his collection of microwavable socks. Of all the people I've ever met, he's one of them.
